Flight Risk Chili by Tana Harter

Description

This is the recipe that got me the win in 2018.

Ingredients

    Stock

  • 3 LB Tri-tip, 1/4 to 3/8 inch diced
  • 1 OZ. Crown Royal “Northern Harvest” Rye
  • 28 OZ. Swanson beef stock
  • 2 TBL Better Than Bouillon chicken base
  • 1 TBL RealSalt sea salt
  • 3 OZ. Hunts tomato sauce

    • Spice dump 1

  • 2 TBL Pendery's medium roast ground chilies
  • 1 TBL Pendery's onion powder
  • 1 TSP Pendery's garlic powder
  • 1/2 TSP Pendery's white pepper

    • Spice dump 2

  • 3 TBL Pendery's red ground chilies
  • 1/2 TBL All Things Chili: Deadly Dudley chili powder
  • 2 TSP Pendery's garlic powder
  • 1 TSP Pendery's Mexican oregano
  • 2 TSP Domino brown sugar

    • Spice dump 3

  • 4 TBL Pendery's quality chili powder
  • 1 1/2 TBL Mild Bill's cumin
  • 1 TBL All Things Chili cumin
  • 2 TSP Wegmans apple cider vinegar

    • As needed

  • RealSalt sea salt, to taste
  • Argo cornstarch, to thicken as needed

Steps

  1. Pour 1 oz. of Crown Royal Rye and make a toast to your friends and family.
  2. Combine all stock ingredients and begin to heat.
  3. Brown tri-tip and add to stock along with Spice Dump 1. Cook for 90 minutes.
  4. Add Spice Dump 2. Continue cooking for 15 minutes.
  5. Add Spice Dump 3. Continue cooking for 45 minutes.
  6. As necessary, add cornstarch to thicken and/or salt to taste.
